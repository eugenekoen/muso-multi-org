Music In His Name

Hey there! Welcome to the app.

If you’ve ever walked onto a stage only to realize you’ve lost your chord sheet or that the song is in a key you aren't prepared for, you’re not alone. As a musician, I’ve been there—and that’s exactly why I built Music In His Name.

What began as a personal hobby to help me stay organized has grown into a passion project: a dedicated app designed specifically for church bands to manage their setlists and music digitally.

Why I built this:

I’m the type of person who needs to see the chords clearly when I play. I found that transposing on the fly often led to mistakes, and paper copies were far too easy to lose. Using reliable open-source technology, I’ve developed a platform that allows you to:

  • Transpose Instantly: Change keys on the fly to match your worship leader’s preference for the week.
  • Dynamic Setlists: Easily add, remove, and reorder songs for any service.
  • Complete Customization: Edit and store your songs digitally so they are ready whenever you are.

This journey wouldn't have been possible without the feedback from my muso friends over the years—with a very special thanks to my uncle, Peter, for his invaluable insights.

My heart behind this is to make the technical side of worship seamless so we can focus on what truly matters.
